From Heart to Plate

The journey of the Gomez Family and how Panyeros Kusina came to life

Rooted in Tradition, Served with Passion

Established in 2024, Panyeros Kusina is a small Filipino restaurant born from a lifelong dream and a passion for authentic cuisine. Our founder, a seasoned chef with experience in five-star hotel kitchens, finally embraced the opportunity to bring his expertise closer to home by opening this family-run eatery. At Panyeros Kusina, every dish tells a story. From rich and hearty Overload Pares to crispy, flavorful hot meals Silogs, our menu reflects the best of Filipino tradition, carefully crafted with a touch of culinary excellence. Beyond the food, our team is truly a family effort—our father leads as head chef, our mother manages the cashier, and the younger generation provides attentive service to every guest. More than just a restaurant, Panyeros Kusina is a place where community and family come together. We take pride in offering not only delicious meals but also a warm and welcoming atmosphere where everyone feels at home. Visit us and experience the heart of Filipino cuisine, served with love and dedication.

Panyeros Kusina may be small in size, but it’s big on flavor and heart. Nestled in the heart of Mabalacat, our humble space brings you the warmth of Filipino home-cooked meals with a cozy, welcoming vibe. Whether you’re dropping by for a quick silog or gathering for comfort food with loved ones, every plate is prepared with care, tradition, and a touch of love — just like family.

At Panyeros Kusina, every dish is a celebration of Filipino comfort food. From hearty silog meals to our mouthwatering best-seller — the Overload Pares — we bring you flavors that feel like home. Craving something grilled? Our juicy liempo hits the spot every time. For sharing and gatherings, enjoy our crowd-favorites like Pansit Bihon, sizzling sisig, and many more. Whether you're dining solo or with your barkada, we serve food that’s always hot, generous, and made with love.
